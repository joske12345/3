Skills and Subjects for Software Development


Welcome to My Software Development:
This website is a showcase of my completed projects and skills acquired during my time at Templemore College of Further Education.
I built this website using HTML and CSS, the basics of web development. However, creating a functional and attractive website involves
more than just coding. It requires a deep understanding of the underlying principles and concepts of software development.


Key Subjects:
The modules I completed for this webpage project include Mobile Technologies, Software Architecture, and Work Experience. These subjects
are essential to the course and have helped me gain a deeper understanding of software development. Mobile Technologies taught me how to
design and develop mobile applications that are user-friendly and efficient. Software Architecture showed me how to design and implement
software systems that are scalable, maintainable, and efficient. Work Experience gave me the opportunity to apply the skills I learned in
the course to real-world projects and scenarios.


Tools:
The Tools page on this website features a BMI calculator, which I built as part of the course. This tool demonstrates my ability to apply the
skills I learned in the course to create practical and useful applications. The BMI calculator is a simple yet effective tool that calculates
an individual's body mass index based on their height and weight. It's a great example of how software development can be used to create solutions
that make a positive impact on people's lives.


Why Templemore College?
Choosing to study Software Development at Templemore College was a great decision. The college provides a supportive environment for all students,
and the principal is always available to help whenever needed. The college's commitment to health and safety is evident in its well-maintained facilities,
creating an ideal environment for both study and personal growth. The college's focus on practical skills and real-world applications has given me
the confidence and skills to pursue a career in software development.


My Experience:
Throughout my time at Templemore College, I have had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects and assignments that have helped me develop my
skills and knowledge in software development. From building simple web applications to designing and implementing complex software systems, I have gained
a deep understanding of the principles and concepts of software development. I have also worked with various programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript.


What I have Learned:
One of the most important things I have learned during my time at Templemore College is the importance of problem solving and critical thinking. Software development
is not just about writing code, it is about solving real world problems and creating solutions that make a positive impact on people's lives. I have also learned the

Conclusion:
