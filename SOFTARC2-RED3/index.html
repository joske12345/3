What is pair-Programing ?

Two programmers work together at a single computer. One, the actively writes the code,
while the other, the reviews each line as it is written, providing feedback, suggesting
improvements, and ensuring overall code quality. The two people would be equally skilled
and would each have equal time at the keyboard.

What disadvantages does pair-programming have ?

Pair programming, while beneficial, may have some drawbacks. It can be resource intensive,
requiring two developersto work on a single task, potentially slowing down productivity.
Differences in working styles or personalities might lead to conflicts, and some individuals
may find the constant collaboration exhausting.
