Testing Blue-2 BMI

Brief note on the difference between data and information,

Data for testing BMI refers to the raw measurements of weight and height used in the BMI formula
data is the input, and information is the output. For BMI, the data is the weight and height measurements,
and the information is the calculated BMI value.

Used online/internet calculator

Testing process i used:

Downloading Blue-2 BMI calculator into my phone. Use word document to create table with inputs and outputs.
Test same inputs and outputs in other calculator and compared it.

Data chosen for testing:

Chose this testing for accurasy outputs.(use same inputs).
